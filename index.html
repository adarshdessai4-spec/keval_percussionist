Introduction

Keval Percussionist is a live percussionist, drummer, liquid & led drummer based in Goa & has been involved in the world of drumming & percussions since the year 2004. He holds various degrees from the Trinity College of Music based in London, England.

In no time he had realised that it is not only the Drumkit he is well equipped with, but also playing various percussion instruments such as Congas, Bongos, Djembe, Darbuka, Roto toms, Timbales, etc.

His act consists of playing various percussion instruments at a time alongside different genres of music played by the Dj.

He has played across all the renowned resorts & clubs of Goa & India for various events such as Weddings, Conferences, Sundowners & Nightlife Gigs.
