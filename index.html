About KKSonic

KKSonic Percussionist is a premium live percussionist and drummer based in India, known for cinematic stage energy and tight grooves. Trained with degrees from Trinity College London, he blends acoustic finesse with bold electronic textures.

From liquid & LED drumming to Afro, Latin and club-ready percussion, KKSonic layers congas, bongos, djembe, darbuka, roto toms and timbales alongside DJ-driven sets to elevate every crowd moment.

He has delivered headlining experiences across luxury weddings, beach sundowners, nightlife residencies and corporate showcases, keeping dancefloors engaged from the first hit to the final drop.
